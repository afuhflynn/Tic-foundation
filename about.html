About TiC Foundation

Empowering the next generation of innovators through technology, entrepreneurship, and education.

Overview

The TiC Foundation leverages technology to create innovative solutions to everyday challenges in African communities.

Our mission is to inspire and equip Cameroon’s next generation of tech entrepreneurs with the digital and business skills they need to succeed.

Our Flagship Projects

  1. The TiC Startup Accelerator Program (SAP): Accelerates growth of early-stage tech startups in Cameroon.
  2. The TiC Summit: A national tech innovation challenge for young innovators in secondary schools.
  3. The TiC Bootcamp: Training that equips young Cameroonians with vital tech skills.
