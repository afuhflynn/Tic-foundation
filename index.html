TIC SUMMIT 2025 RECAP

The TIC summit 2023 edition was a great success. Over 500 volunteers and some staff not numerous outreaches in the Centre, Littoral, West, North West and South West regions of the country. These outreaches got 1500 students pre-registering from the 26 different schools visited. After follow up sessions with the pre-registered, the summit had 500 successful registrations. During the 3 days program, the participants had trainings on Design thinking, Business model Canvas, UI UX designing, Tinker CAD, time management, cybersecurity, branding and marketing, content creation and video editing. These trainings were held at Gingko Fab labs and the High Tech printing center at Polytechnic Yaounde.
